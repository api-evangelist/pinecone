> Stop talking about "AI Performance" as a single metric. To build successful RAG applications, teams must distinguish between Infrastructure Performance (latency, throughput, cost) and Result Quality (accuracy, relevance). This post explores why these two "scoreboards" often move in opposite directions and how to measure them independently to avoid the common pitfalls of AI optimization.
