Pinecone's Nexus product addresses enterprise AI's shift in focus from capability to cost and reliability by compiling structured knowledge artifacts before queries arrive rather than assembling them dynamically at query time. The company benchmarked Nexus against traditional agentic RAG across three customer deployments — patent search, M&A due diligence, and revenue intelligence — demonstrating 85-97% token cost reduction, 18-77% faster latency, and accuracy gains of 14-94%. These real-world results from early access customers validate the compile-time knowledge architecture as a production-ready alternative to query-time retrieval pipelines.
