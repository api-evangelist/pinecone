Pinecone Assistant now has support for new OpenAI (gpt-4.1, o4-mini) and Anthropic (claude-3-7-sonnet) models, as well as Gemini (gemini-2.5-pro). In addition to the new selection of models, Pinecone Assistant now gives developers more control by exposing the temperature parameter for the chosen LLM.
