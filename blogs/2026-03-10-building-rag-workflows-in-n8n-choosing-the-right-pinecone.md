For most n8n builders, the Pinecone Assistant node is the right starting point. When you hit a real limitation — specialized content that needs custom chunking, a domain-specific embedding model, advanced retrieval techniques — the Pinecone Vector Store node gives you the control to go deeper. This article will help you choose the right node.
